Python bot on World Energy - Analysis

The February 2022 war in Ukraine continues to have far reaching impact on food, energy and finance. Specifically, the Energy Markets went bunkers right from the war's inception, with spikes in both oil and gas prices, further aggravated by ensuing energy-related western sanctions on Russia. This has ultimately led to increased conversations around energy security and the need for strategic investments in renewable energy. This robot was built to understand the world's historical and current energy trends and electricity mix. The bot also assesses country-specific energy and electricity information and has been built flexible to accommodate additional reference points per country and obtainable energy scope.
